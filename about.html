CMC Global

The strategic unit of CMC Corporation, was established on 31 March 2017

The reason of establishment

CMC Global was founded from 25 years of ICT experience and over 10 years of Outsourcing field of CMC Corp. The company’s mission is to provide competent software engineers, ICT solutions and services for the overseas markets. At present, CMC Global is employing more than 300 staff together with a member company in Japan.

Core Value

CMC Corp and CMC Global are honored to be one of Vietnam leading enterprises in the field of providing and developing software solutions and services. To fulfill the prerequisite criteria for the development of CMC Corp: Creativity, Professionalism and Teamwork, CMC Global always strives to build up a strong community, to be on top in Vietnam technology industry and reach further to the world.

Future vision

With the aim of bringing CMC’s high – tech products and services internationally, CMC Global is making efforts to set up the first foundations with the ambitions: having at least 1000 employees working overseas by 2020, revenue from software products and services to the foreign markets will surpass the domestic; more products and services meet the world – class standards. In the future, CMC Global plans to open more subsidiaries in some Asian countries such as Singapore, Malaysia.

Development strategy

CMC Global’s investment strategies are planned differently compared to other Vietnamese IT outsourcing firms. CMC Global will concentrate on developing resources, standardizing the process of service provision and software innovation, training high – quality human resources, and at the same time attracting a large number of ICT talents. CMC Global will also play the role of a representative connecting all CMC member companies in providing software solutions and packaged integration services to the world.
